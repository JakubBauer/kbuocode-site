Idle Merge Farm

Idle Merge Farm is a casual Android game about merging animals, improving a farm, collecting coins and gems, and unlocking steady progression over short sessions.

Features

  • 4x4 merge board with animal progression.
  • Coins, gems, chests, tasks, missions, upgrades, and prestige.
  • Optional rewarded ads for bonuses and chests.
  • Local save and lightweight idle progression.
