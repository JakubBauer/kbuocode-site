Bomb Defuse

Bomb Defuse is a quick reaction game where players cut the right wire before the timer runs out. It is built for short sessions, fast choices, and growing pressure.

Features

  • Fast rounds focused on reflexes and decision making.
  • Classic and challenge-style gameplay.
  • Local progress, records, achievements, and unlocks.
  • Advertising-supported mobile game experience.
