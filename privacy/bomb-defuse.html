Privacy Policy - Bomb Defuse

Last updated: May 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Bomb Defuse handles information when you use the mobile game. Bomb Defuse is published by KbuO Code.

Information stored on your device

Bomb Defuse stores gameplay-related information locally on your device. This may include audio settings, gameplay preferences, selected mode and difficulty, best scores, gameplay statistics, coins, achievements, unlocked cosmetics, and challenge progress. This data is used to make the game work properly and preserve your progress on that device.

Information not directly collected by KbuO Code

KbuO Code does not require you to create an account and does not directly ask for your name, address, phone number, contacts, photos, precise location, or microphone recordings. The game does not include chat, user profiles, or a developer-owned account system.

Advertising, analytics, and third-party services

Bomb Defuse may use Google AdMob to display ads, including interstitial ads and rewarded ads. It may also use Firebase services such as Firebase Analytics to understand app usage and stability.

When these services are used, Google and its partners may process data needed to provide, limit, measure, secure, and report on ads and app usage. This may include advertising identifiers, IP address, device and app information, diagnostics, performance data, analytics events, and ad interaction data.

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy, Google Mobile Ads information at https://developers.google.com/admob/android/privacy, and Firebase privacy information at https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

How information is used

  • To save game progress and settings on your device.
  • To provide scores, statistics, achievements, and unlocks.
  • To display, measure, and improve advertising.
  • To understand app usage, stability, and performance.

Sharing of information

KbuO Code does not sell your personal information. Limited technical data may be processed by third-party services such as Google AdMob and Firebase where necessary for advertising, analytics, stability, and app functionality. Local gameplay data is not uploaded to a dedicated KbuO Code server.

Permissions and device features

Bomb Defuse may use internet access to load advertising content and online service functionality. It may also use audio and vibration features for gameplay feedback. Bomb Defuse does not record, store, or transmit microphone audio for gameplay.

Data retention

Local gameplay data remains on your device until you clear the app storage, uninstall the app, or the data is overwritten by normal gameplay. Data handled by third-party services is governed by their own policies and retention practices.

Your choices

You can stop using the app at any time, clear local app storage, or uninstall the app. Advertising and analytics controls may be available in your device settings and through Google's privacy controls.

Children's privacy

Bomb Defuse is a general-audience game and is not intended to knowingly collect personal information directly from children. If the app is used by younger audiences, parents or guardians should review device and Google Play privacy settings.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be made available on this page.
